Parkolay & Alupco
Partnership

PARKOLAY and ALUPCO are joining forces to transform parking solutions in Saudi Arabia. PARKOLAY brings cutting-edge automated parking systems, while ALUPCO contributes its expertise in manufacturing. This partnership aligns with Saudi Vision 2030, aiming to enhance urban infrastructure and address congestion issues in Saudi cities.​

Company Overview:
Parkolay

For over 27 years, Parkolay has been delivering forward-thinking and innovative solutions in automated parking systems. With our R&D center and production facility, we continue to grow by creating hundreds of parking spaces worldwide. Through successful projects in countries like the USA, Canada, Spain, Australia, Germany, and many others, we deliver innovative parking solutions across the globe.​

Company Overview:
Alupco

Founded in 1975, Aluminium Products Company (ALUPCO) stands as the leading aluminium extrusion company in the Middle East and North African region (MENA). Headquarters is located in Dammam, with an additional plant in Jeddah with a total capacity exceeding 85,000 MT per year, ALUPCO offers a comprehensive range of cutting-edge aluminium products and innovative building solutions to our customers worldwide.​
