Parkolay & Alupco
Partnership

PARKOLAY and ALUPCO are joining forces to transform parking solutions in Saudi Arabia. PARKOLAY brings cutting-edge automated parking systems, while ALUPCO contributes its expertise in manufacturing. This partnership aligns with Saudi Vision 2030, aiming to enhance urban infrastructure and address congestion issues in Saudi cities.​
